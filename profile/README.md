# CIMPA — Centro de Investigación en Matemática Pura y Aplicada

**Research center in pure and applied mathematics at the
Universidad de Costa Rica**

---

## About

CIMPA (Centro de Investigación en Matemática Pura y Aplicada) is
a research unit of the Universidad de Costa Rica, founded in
1997. It brings together mathematicians working across a broad
spectrum of pure and applied areas, and serves as the main hub
for mathematical research in Costa Rica and the wider Central
American region.

CIMPA hosts graduate programs, organizes seminars and
conferences, and maintains active collaborations with
institutions across Latin America, Europe, and North America.

## Research Areas

- **Analysis & Differential Equations**
- **Numerical Analysis & Optimization**
- **Algebra & Number Theory**
- **Statistics & Biological Mathematics**
- **Geometry & Topology**
- **Logic**
- **Probability & Combinatorics**
- **Mathematical Biology & Epidemiology**
